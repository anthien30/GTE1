About Me

Favorites

Mangoniada

If you have ever tried mangoniada, my favorite type of dessert, you will find that it consists of several different flavors and textures. It's a little spicy, a little sour, a little sweet, a little fluid, a little powdery, a little icy, ...
A bit of everything combines together to produce something fantastically tasty!

Autumn

Fall is my favorite season of the year. There's just something about it that gives me a very peaceful feeling, although mildly sad. Something in the orangeness of fallen leaves, in the cracking of withered branches, and in the lullaby of the autumn breeze that carries nature to a deep sleep, awaiting new life in the next spring.

Hobbies

One thing I like to do in my free time is drawing. As a person who loves animals, I focus most of my art on them. Drawing helps me gain patience, the ability to observe and pay attention to detail, as well as creativity.
Reading manga is one of the things that I enjoy doing the most! This hobby doesn't just help me relax but also gets dangerous as I often get too carried away reading them. Through reading these mangas, I learn about random interesting things without even realizing!
